Empowering Technicians

GearUp is a grassroots initiative driving social mobility by donating essential vehicles to local technicians, service workers, and entrepreneurs. We believe transportation isn’t just a convenience—it’s a catalyst for dignity, independence, and sustainable income. Whether it's a pickup loaded with tools or a bike that powers a delivery route, GearUp equips hardworking individuals with the means to move forward—literally.

About Our Initiative

Building a better Tomorrow

Mission

Empowering the working indivuduals to develop independency

Vision

We aim to help individuals start or grow their own ventures by giving them reliable transportation tools needed to deliver their services and goods efficiently.

Campaign

GearUp is more than a campaign—it's a movement putting wheels under dreams.

Our Aim

Empowering lives with mobility—one pickup, moped, or bicycle at a time.

  • The GearUp initiative aims to empower technicians, tradespeople, and micro-entrepreneurs by providing them with essential vehicles—such as used pickup trucks, mopeds, and bicycles—to support their livelihood and mobility. By removing logistical barriers, the project enables individuals to access job sites, deliver services, and grow small businesses. It focuses on sustainable impact by recycling functional vehicles with at least three years of operational life remaining. The initiative prioritizes dignity-driven aid, turning donations into tools for long-term economic independence. Ultimately, GearUp supports local resilience by fueling opportunity and independency.
